Wolfgang Mozart

Johannes Chrystostomus Wolfgangus Theophilus Mozart was bord in 1756 and passed away in 1791. He is considered to be one of the most gifted musicians of all time. He composed many great pieces of classical music. Mozart was more than a gifted composer though. He was also a conductor, pianist, organist, and violinist. Mozart made music for opera, symphony, concerto, instrumental, vocal and many more.
