Who is George R. R. Martin?

George R.R. Martin was born September 20, 1948. He began writing stories at a very young age and would sell them to other neighborhood children for pennies. In high school, George became a fan of comic books and would start a collection and even wrote fan magazines. George's first ever sale in his professional career was at the age of 21 where he sold "The Hero" to Galaxy for publishing. He would later write "A Song of Ice and Fire" which would become one of his best selling books and would receive a T.V. series adaption by HBO.
